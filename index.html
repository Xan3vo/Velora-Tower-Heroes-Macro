Guide ×
1 Install AutoHotkey v1.1 — not v2, they're incompatible. The app detects it automatically and tells you at launch if it's missing.
2 Set Windows display scale to 100% and a supported resolution. Right-click desktop → Display settings. The macro clicks exact pixels, so both are checked before every run.
3 Pick your map & mode, then hit Play (or press F1). Roblox launches into a private server and the macro takes over — no setup inside the game needed.
4 Walk away. The macro places heroes, upgrades them, and loops rounds until you stop it. A floating overlay shows live Mana / Rounds / Coins / XP while it runs.

⚠️ While the macro runs, don't move the mouse or type — it's clicking real pixels on your screen. Keep Roblox fullscreen on your main monitor. Press F3 any time to take back control.
Launch & lobby Opens a private server, waits for Roblox to go fullscreen, clicks through the lobby and readies up.
Gather mana Collects mana for ~30 seconds and lines up the camera before placing anything.
Place heroes Places the map's hero loadout, then verifies each hero card shows as placed — retrying only the ones that are missing.
Upgrade to MAX Upgrades each hero until its button reads MAX — checked by reading the actual button text off the screen, with a pixel-color fallback.
Finish & loop Waits for the map-complete screen, reads your coin & XP rewards off the results dialog, returns to the lobby, and starts the next round.

Safety nets

🔁Auto-recovery
If Roblox crashes, disconnects, or a step times out, the macro closes everything and relaunches from scratch on its own.
🛑Restart-loop guard
5 restarts in a row without completing a round means something's actually broken — the macro stops itself instead of relaunching Roblox forever. Toggle in Settings → General.
1 In Discord, open your server and pick a channel for updates. Channel settings (⚙) → Integrations → Webhooks → New Webhook → Copy Webhook URL.
2 Paste the URL in Settings → Discord tab and hit Test. A green test embed should appear in your channel within a second.
3 Pick which events get posted with the toggles below the URL. Start/stop, round results, restarts & errors are on by default; placements and upgrades are off (they're chatty).
4 Optional: add your Discord user ID to get @pinged on critical alerts only. Discord → Settings → Advanced → Developer Mode on, then right-click your name → Copy User ID. Pings fire for the restart-loop guard and errors — never routine messages.
💡 Every round you get an embed with round + total coins/XP, mana, elapsed time and restart count — so you can check on a long farm session from your phone without touching the PC.
🔍Stuck on “Looking for play button”
Almost always display settings: Windows scale must be 100% and resolution one of the three supported ones. Also make sure Roblox is fullscreen on your main monitor and nothing is covering it.
🪙Coins or XP show 0 / wrong numbers
The reward reader scans fixed screen regions. If yours misread, open Settings → Advanced, use the per-region Test buttons with the results screen visible, nudge the boxes, or hit Reset to defaults.
🔁It keeps restarting Roblox
One or two restarts per session is normal recovery (bad server, slow load). Constant restarts usually mean wrong resolution/scale — and after 5 in a row without a finished round, the loop guard stops the macro automatically.
🌐A roblox.com browser tab is left open each launch
That's how Roblox launches. Turn on Close leftover browser tab in Settings → General and the macro closes it for you once the game is up.
⌨️Hotkeys do nothing
They're global, but another app may have grabbed F1/F3/F4 first. Close overlay tools (Xbox Game Bar, screen recorders) or use the in-app buttons.
🛑How do I stop it?
Press F3 anywhere, or click Stop in the app. With Close Roblox on stop enabled (Settings → General), Roblox exits too.
